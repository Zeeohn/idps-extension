Action Required: Update Your Graduation Picture

To make sure your graduation picture is included in all official documentation and ceremonies, we kindly request that you update your picture on the school portal before Wednesday, 16th August 2023. Your updated picture will not only be featured on your graduation certificate but will also be proudly displayed during the graduation ceremony.

Instructions to Update Your Picture:

  1. Log in to your student portal at https://uilugportal.unilorin.edu.ng/login.php.
  2. Navigate to the "Personal Details" section.
  3. Click on the "Final Year Student Passport Upload" option.
  4. Follow the guidelines for uploading a high-quality, clear, and professional portrait photo.
  5. Save the changes to ensure your new picture is successfully updated.
